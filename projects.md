## Sell‑Out Customer Analytics Platform  
**Internal product · Enterprise commercial analytics**

### Context  
Commercial and product teams lacked a unified view of customer performance across markets, limiting their ability to understand acquisition, churn, and behavioral drivers.

### My Role  
Lead Data Scientist - ownership of problem framing, analytical design, and delivery in close collaboration with product and commercial stakeholders.

### Solution  
Designed and implemented an analytics product combining:
- customer segmentation
- behavioral and lifecycle metrics
- predictive indicators
- self‑serve dashboards for non‑technical users

The platform was built to balance **interpretability, scalability, and decision relevance** rather than purely model performance.

### Impact  
- Adopted by **5+ enterprise clients** (global consumer healthcare / FMCG companies)  
- Generated **€200K+ in revenue**  
- Reduced ad‑hoc analysis requests through self‑serve access  

### Key Decisions  
- Prioritized transparent metrics trusted by stakeholders over black‑box approaches  
- Designed analytics explicitly around **commercial decision‑making**  
- Ensured reproducibility and scalability of data pipelines  

**Skills & Tools**  
Python · SQL · Product Analytics · Predictive Modeling · Power BI · Streamlit · Azure / Cloudera

## Drug Stock‑Out Prediction Model  
**Internal product · Predictive modeling**

### Context  
Unexpected competitor drug shortages created sudden market shifts that commercial teams could not anticipate or react to in time.

### My Role  
Data Scientist responsible for model development, evaluation strategy, and production integration.

### Solution  
Developed a predictive model to anticipate drug stock‑outs, integrating historical supply signals and market indicators to surface early‑warning alerts.

### Impact  
- Enabled commercial teams to **react proactively** to anticipated shortages  
- Helped protect market share during competitive supply disruptions  

### Key Decisions  
- Chose evaluation metrics aligned with **early detection**, not just accuracy  
- Designed models to support **actionable lead time**, not post‑hoc explanations  
- Balanced model complexity with operational reliability  

**Skills & Tools**  
Python · Time Series Modeling · Feature Engineering · Model Evaluation · Production Pipelines
